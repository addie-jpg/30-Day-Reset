Day 14: Read for Pleasure

Why Read?

Reading stimulates your mind and provides relaxation.

How to Read

  1. Choose a book or article you enjoy.
  2. Set aside 20-30 minutes uninterrupted.
  3. Read actively, savoring the words and ideas.

Tips

  • Pick a cozy spot with good lighting.
  • Explore different genres and topics.

Reflection Questions

  • Did reading help you relax or focus?
  • What did you enjoy most?

Encouragement

Feed your mind and imagination every day!

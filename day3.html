Day 3: Healthy Breakfast

Why a Healthy Breakfast?

Eating a nutritious breakfast fuels your body and mind for the day.

How to Make It

  1. Choose whole grains, fruits, and protein.
  2. Examples: oatmeal with berries, yogurt and nuts, eggs with veggies.
  3. Avoid sugary cereals and processed foods.

Tips

  • Prepare ingredients the night before.
  • Experiment with new recipes.

Reflection Questions

  • How did your breakfast affect your energy?
  • Did you enjoy trying something new?

Encouragement

Your body deserves the best start—keep nourishing it!

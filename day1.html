Day 1: Morning Mindfulness

Why Mindfulness?

Starting your day with mindfulness sets a calm tone, reduces stress, and increases awareness.

How to Practice

  1. Find a quiet spot and sit comfortably.
  2. Close your eyes and focus on your breath for 5 minutes.
  3. Notice thoughts but let them pass without judgment.
  4. Open your eyes gently and start your day intentionally.

Tips

  • Use a timer or a meditation app.
  • If your mind wanders, gently bring it back to your breath.

Reflection Questions

  • How did mindfulness affect your morning?
  • Did you notice changes in stress or focus?

Encouragement

Starting small creates lasting habits. Keep practicing!

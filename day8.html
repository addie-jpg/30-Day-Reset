Day 8: Walk Outside

Why Walk Outside?

Nature improves mood, creativity, and physical health.

How to Enjoy Your Walk

  1. Choose a safe, pleasant route.
  2. Walk 15-20 minutes at a comfortable pace.
  3. Notice sights, sounds, and smells.
  4. Limit phone use to photos or leave it behind.

Tips

  • Wear comfortable shoes and weather-appropriate clothes.
  • Walk with a friend or family member if you like.

Reflection Questions

  • How did nature affect your mood?
  • What did you notice you usually miss?
  • Would you make this a habit?

Encouragement

Nature is a powerful healer. Step outside and soak it in!

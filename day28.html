Day 28: Plan a Mini Adventure

Why Adventure?

New experiences boost excitement and growth.

How to Plan

  1. Choose a nearby place you’ve never explored.
  2. Set a date and gather what you need.
  3. Go with an open mind and enjoy!

Tips

  • Keep it simple and flexible.
  • Invite a friend or go solo.

Reflection Questions

  • What did you discover?
  • How did it feel to try something new?

Encouragement

Adventure awaits—go find it!

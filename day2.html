Day 2: Gratitude Journal

Why Gratitude?

Writing gratitude increases happiness and reduces negativity.

How to Journal

  1. Grab a notebook or journal.
  2. Write down 3 things you are grateful for today.
  3. Reflect on why you appreciate these things.

Tips

  • Be specific rather than general.
  • Try to find new things each day.

Reflection Questions

  • How did writing gratitude affect your mood?
  • Did it change your perspective on your day?

Encouragement

Gratitude shifts your mindset—keep it flowing!

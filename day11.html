Day 11: Compliment Yourself

Why Compliment Yourself?

Self-kindness boosts confidence and resilience.

How to Compliment

  1. Look in a mirror and say 3 things you like about yourself.
  2. Focus on character traits, efforts, or strengths.
  3. Write them down in a journal for lasting effect.

Tips

  • Be sincere and specific.
  • Repeat daily to build positive self-talk.

Reflection Questions

  • How did this exercise feel?
  • Did it change your inner dialogue?

Encouragement

You deserve kindness — especially from yourself!

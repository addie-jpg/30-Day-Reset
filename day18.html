Day 18: Practice Gratitude

Why Gratitude?

Gratitude improves mood and perspective.

How to Practice

  1. Write down 3 things you’re grateful for today.
  2. Reflect on why each is meaningful.
  3. Share gratitude with someone if you can.

Tips

  • Be specific and heartfelt.
  • Make it a daily habit.

Reflection Questions

  • Did gratitude shift your mood?
  • What new perspectives arose?

Encouragement

Gratitude turns what we have into enough.

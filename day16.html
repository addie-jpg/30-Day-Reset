Day 16: Write a Letter to Your Future Self

Why Write?

Reflecting on your goals and dreams encourages growth.

How to Write

  1. Find a quiet space and set a timer for 15 minutes.
  2. Write a letter to yourself 1 year from now.
  3. Include hopes, encouragement, and reminders.

Tips

  • Be honest and compassionate.
  • Keep the letter somewhere safe.

Reflection Questions

  • What did you learn about yourself?
  • How can this letter inspire your actions?

Encouragement

Your future self will thank you for this gift!

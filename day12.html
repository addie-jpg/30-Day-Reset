Day 12: Drink Herbal Tea

Why Herbal Tea?

Herbal teas soothe, hydrate, and support relaxation.

How to Enjoy

  1. Choose a caffeine-free herbal tea like chamomile or peppermint.
  2. Brew according to instructions and savor the aroma.
  3. Drink slowly, focusing on the warmth and flavor.

Tips

  • Make it a calming ritual, especially before bed.
  • Try different flavors to find favorites.

Reflection Questions

  • Did the tea help you relax?
  • How did you feel afterward?

Encouragement

Small rituals add peace. Enjoy your tea time!

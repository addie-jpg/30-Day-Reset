Day 22: Meditate for 10 Minutes

Why Meditate?

Meditation reduces stress and improves focus.

How to Meditate

  1. Find a quiet, comfortable place.
  2. Set a timer for 10 minutes.
  3. Focus on your breath or a calming word.

Tips

  • Don’t worry about "clearing your mind."
  • Be gentle with yourself if your thoughts wander.

Reflection Questions

  • How did meditation affect your mood?
  • Did you notice any changes in focus?

Encouragement

Ten minutes can change your day—try it!

Day 9: Unplug for 1 Hour

Why Unplug?

Taking a break from screens reduces eye strain and mental fatigue.

How to Unplug

  1. Choose a 1-hour window.
  2. Turn off/silence phones, computers, TV, tablets.
  3. Use time to read, journal, walk, or connect with loved ones.
  4. Notice how you feel without digital distractions.

Tips

  • Inform others you’ll be offline.
  • Plan activities beforehand.

Reflection Questions

  • What was challenging or enjoyable about unplugging?
  • Did your mood or focus change?
  • Would you schedule unplugged time regularly?

Encouragement

Disconnect to reconnect with yourself and others.

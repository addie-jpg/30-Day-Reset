Day 24: Do a Random Act of Kindness

Why Kindness?

Acts of kindness improve your mood and others’.

How to Act Kindly

  1. Do something thoughtful for someone else today.
  2. It could be a compliment, a favor, or a small gift.
  3. Notice how it makes you feel.

Tips

  • Be genuine and unobtrusive.
  • Kindness is contagious — spread it!

Reflection Questions

  • What did you do?
  • How did kindness affect you and others?

Encouragement

Small kindnesses make a big difference.

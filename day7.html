Day 7: Declutter One Area

Why Declutter?

Tidying one area clears your mind and reduces stress.

How to Declutter

  1. Choose one area (desk, drawer, shelf, etc.)
  2. Remove everything from it.
  3. Sort items into keep, donate, trash, relocate.
  4. Organize kept items neatly back.
  5. Dispose of unwanted items promptly.

Tips

  • Set a 20-30 minute timer.
  • Play music or a podcast to stay motivated.

Reflection Questions

  • How did decluttering affect your mood?
  • What did you learn about your belongings?
  • What area to tackle next?

Encouragement

Small wins lead to big changes. Celebrate your progress!

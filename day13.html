Day 13: Connect with a Friend

Why Connect?

Social connection improves mental health and happiness.

How to Connect

  1. Reach out via call, text, or in person.
  2. Ask how they’re doing and share your own news.
  3. Plan a future meet-up or activity together.

Tips

  • Be genuine and listen actively.
  • Make time for meaningful conversations.

Reflection Questions

  • How did connecting make you feel?
  • Did it strengthen your relationship?

Encouragement

Relationships nourish your soul. Keep them strong!

Day 21: Spend Time Outdoors

Why Outdoors?

Nature boosts mood, energy, and creativity.

How to Spend Time

  1. Go for a walk, hike, or sit in a park.
  2. Notice the sights, sounds, and smells around you.
  3. Breathe deeply and relax your mind.

Tips

  • Dress for comfort and weather.
  • Bring water and a snack if needed.

Reflection Questions

  • How did being outdoors affect your mood?
  • What did you enjoy most about it?

Encouragement

Let nature refresh and inspire you!

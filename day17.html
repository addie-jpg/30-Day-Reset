Day 17: Declutter a Space

Why Declutter?

A tidy space promotes calm and productivity.

How to Declutter

  1. Choose one small area (desk, drawer, shelf).
  2. Sort items into keep, donate, or toss piles.
  3. Organize what remains neatly.

Tips

  • Set a timer for 20-30 minutes.
  • Be honest about what you really use.

Reflection Questions

  • How did the space feel afterward?
  • Did decluttering clear your mind?

Encouragement

Clear space, clear mind—keep it up!

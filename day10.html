Day 10: Plan Your Day

Why Plan?

Planning increases productivity and reduces stress.

How to Plan

  1. Choose a notebook, planner, or app.
  2. Write your top 3 priorities for the day.
  3. Include breaks and self-care.
  4. Review and adjust as needed.

Tips

  • Be realistic and flexible.
  • Celebrate small wins.

Reflection Questions

  • Did planning help focus your day?
  • What worked or didn’t?

Encouragement

You’re in charge of your day. Own it!

Day 20: Unplug for an Hour

Why Unplug?

Reducing screen time helps reset focus and calm.

How to Unplug

  1. Choose an hour without phones, computers, or TV.
  2. Use this time to relax, read, or be outside.
  3. Notice how it feels to disconnect.

Tips

  • Plan this time ahead to avoid stress.
  • Invite others to join for support.

Reflection Questions

  • What was hardest about unplugging?
  • Did it help you feel refreshed?

Encouragement

Disconnect to reconnect with yourself!

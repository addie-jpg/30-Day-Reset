Day 25: Listen to Uplifting Music

Why Music?

Music boosts mood, energy, and motivation.

How to Listen

  1. Choose your favorite uplifting songs or playlists.
  2. Listen attentively, maybe sing or dance along.
  3. Let the music energize you.

Tips

  • Create a go-to playlist for tough days.
  • Try new genres to expand your mood toolkit.

Reflection Questions

  • How did the music affect your mood?
  • Did you feel more energized or calm?

Encouragement

Let music lift your spirit anytime!

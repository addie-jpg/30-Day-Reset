Day 15: Try a New Hobby

Why New Hobbies?

Exploring new activities sparks creativity and joy.

How to Try

  1. Pick something you’re curious about (drawing, cooking, knitting, etc.)
  2. Set aside 30 minutes to explore it.
  3. Allow yourself to be a beginner without pressure.

Tips

  • Use online tutorials or local classes.
  • Invite a friend to join for fun.

Reflection Questions

  • How did trying something new feel?
  • Would you continue this hobby?

Encouragement

New experiences enrich your life. Keep exploring!

Day 4: Stretch and Move

Why Stretch?

Stretching reduces muscle tension, improves circulation, and boosts energy.

How to Stretch

  1. Spend 5-10 minutes stretching major muscle groups.
  2. Include neck, shoulders, back, legs, and arms.
  3. Hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds without bouncing.

Tips

  • Do it in the morning or after sitting for a long time.
  • Breathe deeply during stretches.

Reflection Questions

  • How did stretching affect your body and mood?
  • Did you feel more relaxed or energized?

Encouragement

Movement is medicine. Keep your body happy!

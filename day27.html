Day 27: Connect with a Friend

Why Connect?

Social connections support happiness and well-being.

How to Connect

  1. Reach out to a friend by call, message, or in person.
  2. Share how you’re doing and listen actively.
  3. Plan a fun activity together if possible.

Tips

  • Be open and genuine.
  • Reconnect even if it’s been a while.

Reflection Questions

  • How did connecting affect your mood?
  • What did you enjoy about the conversation?

Encouragement

Friends brighten life—reach out today!

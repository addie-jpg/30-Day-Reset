Day 23: Write Down Your Wins

Why Write Wins?

Recognizing successes boosts motivation and confidence.

How to Write Wins

  1. List 3 achievements from today, big or small.
  2. Reflect on what made them possible.
  3. Celebrate yourself for each win.

Tips

  • Be honest and kind in your reflections.
  • Keep a journal for ongoing progress.

Reflection Questions

  • Which win felt most meaningful?
  • How can you build on your successes?

Encouragement

Celebrate progress, no matter the size!

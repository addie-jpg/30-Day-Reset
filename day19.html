Day 19: Try a New Recipe

Why Cook New?

Trying new recipes expands skills and enjoyment.

How to Try

  1. Pick a recipe you’ve never made before.
  2. Gather ingredients and prepare mindfully.
  3. Enjoy the process and the meal.

Tips

  • Choose something simple or adventurous.
  • Invite others to share the meal.

Reflection Questions

  • What did you learn?
  • Would you make this again?

Encouragement

New tastes bring new joy—bon appétit!

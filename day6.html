Day 6: Hydration Check

Why Stay Hydrated?

Proper hydration supports energy, digestion, skin, and mood.

How to Stay Hydrated

  1. Drink at least 8 glasses (2 liters) of water today.
  2. Carry a reusable water bottle.
  3. Include herbal teas or infused water.
  4. Avoid excessive caffeine or sugary drinks.

Tips

  • Set phone reminders to drink.
  • Listen to your body’s thirst signals.

Reflection Questions

  • How much water did you drink?
  • Did you notice changes in energy or mood?

Encouragement

Water is life! Keep sipping and feel refreshed.

Day 29: Reflect on Your Progress

Why Reflect?

Reflection highlights growth and next steps.

How to Reflect

  1. Review your journal or notes from this challenge.
  2. Identify your biggest changes and wins.
  3. Set goals for continuing your growth.

Tips

  • Be honest and gentle with yourself.
  • Celebrate progress, not perfection.

Reflection Questions

  • What surprised you about your journey?
  • What will you keep doing?

Encouragement

Your journey matters—keep going strong!
